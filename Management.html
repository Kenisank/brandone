Executive Management

Salisu A. Abdullahi, Chairman - Board of Directors, BrandOne Technologies Limited. He is a veteran IT professional with experience in system design, development, implementation, operations and support. He served as the Senior Special Assistant on IT to the Government of Kano State and member of the ICT Technical Working Group of the National Integrated Infrastructure Master Plan (NIIMP). He is currently serves an executive in a number of organisations.

Salisu A. Abdullahi graduated from the University of New Haven, Connecticut, USA, with Bachelors and Masters awards in Business Data processing and Computer Information Science. He held various teaching, technical and managerial positions in the United States including Director, Business Development, Essex Management Centre (EMC), Essex, Connecticut, USA.

The chairman has served meritoriously in various capacities for a number of national and international bodies including: National President, Zumunta Association USA, National Assistance Secretary, Nigerian Information Technology Professionals in America (NITPA), Former Board Member, Nigerians in Diaspora Organization (NIDO) Americas, Member, Black Data Processing Associate (BDPA).

The chairman’s vision to deliver state of the art technology initiated a study tour for a 15-man delegation to Silicon Valley, California, USA, under the leadership of the Governor of Kano State. The initiative opened the gates for Bill Gate’s first trip to Nigeria to aid the efforts in the eradication of polio in the country. The chairman has also coordinated management and IT workshops for senior management in the public and private sectors including NNPC, CBN, FIRS and FERMA.
