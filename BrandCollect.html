Approvals, Permits & Licenses.

BrandCollect is a web based service that facilitates real-time collection of payments for ministries, departments and agencies (MDAs) of government. The service provides a web form and a payment gateway for web payment and bank branch payment of government fees, levies and other statutory payments. Revenues are collected and accounted for using the service. It comes with API libraries that interfaces with the Legacy application of MDA.

The service is used for the issuance of statutory Approvals, Permits and Licenses as may be required.
