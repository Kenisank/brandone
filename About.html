About Us

Founded in 2013, BrandOne Technologies Ltd is rapidly growing to becoming a leading provider of Vendor Independent Enterprise Solutions. We translate our customers’ business requirements into IT service delivery, capability maximising ROI using our proprietary project management framework and software development tools.

Through decades of collective experience in enterprise web application designs, implementation and working with global vendors, we saw a gap when it comes to enterprise web applications and system integration. This gap is being filled with our industry certified professionals with over 15 years of hands-on experience in system design, implementation, integration and customer service. This has allowed us to continually deliver excellence in delivery and to nurture ongoing customer relationships. 

We pride ourselves as an organisation with reputation built on trust, ideas, quality and good customer service. We ensure our customers get the best value for their investment in any solution we delivered to them. Our business is built upon strong in-house professionals and healthy customer relationships. We promote a culture of openness, teamwork, collaboration, value add and partnership.
