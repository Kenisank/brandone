CAMPUS-DIRECT

Developed by our teams of professionals.

CampusDirect is an embodiment of various tools required for optimal running of Tertiary Institutions; it comprises a set of enterprise web applications that are individually and collectively designed to aid in proper administration and operations of Tertiary Institutions. The smooth running of the Institution facilitates interaction between the Institution, the lecturers, the students and their sponsors/guardians.  

The suite is composed of two enterprise solutions:  
    • School Fees Payment System;
    • School Administration System.

Features

• School Website.

• Student Application.

• Entrance / Semester Examination Registration.

• Entrance / Semester Examination Result Checking.

• Admission Administration.

• Certificate verification for employers.

• Transcript Management and Confirmations.

• Assignments Management.

• Course Outline / Syllabus Management. 

• Registration for New Students.

• Registration for Returning Students.

• Online Payment and Collection of School Fees.

• Report Generation for Account Department.

• Identity Card Generation.

• Hostel Management.

• Complaints Management.

• Mail Management.

• Computer / Data Center.
