BREMS

Brandone Revenue Enhancement and Monitoring System.

Brandone revenue enhancement and monitoring system (BREMS) - is a suite of application for collection of revenue by federal, state and local

government. The core component of the solution is the payment platform. It connects all the collection centers (banks and other accredited locations) to

a centralsystem using multiple redundant highly available links. 

Objectives
The objective of the solution is not only to increase the benchmark of federal, states or local governments’ IGR, the flowing objectives are expected to be achieved by the system: 

1

Simplification of payment process for rates, levies and taxes.

2

To increase the level of Internally Generated Revenue significantly.

3

Providing state with a comprehensive stratified tax payers data base.

4

To block loopholes and leakages in all aspects of the State’s revenue collection procedures.

5

To ensure that the basis for the State’s Federation Account Allocation is accurate since its IGR is a factor in determining its Federation Account Allocation.

6

Generation of electronic receipts and Tax Clearance Certificate (e-TCC).

7

Use of technology to automate all the processes involved in IGR.

Goals

It is aimed that at the end of the implementation OF BREMS, the following goals will be achieved:

1

Development of an end-to end revenue collection and monitoring portal.

2

Installation of IT facilities such as computers, printers, internet access and generators at the Tax stations.

3

Distribution of customized Terminals/Card readers to designated places and Tax offices.

4

Continuous maintenance of database to ensure data integrity.

5

Staging of vigorous enlightenment campaigns to create public awareness.

6

Training of resource persons for the project.

7

Continuous cost-benefit analysis of the project.

8

Gathering of accurate and timely Taxpayers Data.

9

Provision of logistic support for the project.
