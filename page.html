BRANDPAY

Developed by our teams of professionals.

BRANDPAY is a web based solution with an efficient platform for managing multiple credit payments that recur periodically across banks. It enables organizations perform one-off or recurring direct credit multiple payments to vendors, contractors, suppliers and staff, across multiple banks in a timely and cost effective manner. It can be used for the payment of claims to clients and salaries in a timely manner.

Features & Benefits

1

Use for Salaries, Pension Payments, Contractors/third party companies, Travel expense, Petty cash disbursement, Staff allowances.

2

No Clearing Period as solution is online, real-time access.

3

Eliminate the need for multiple schedules & cheques to be prepared by the Organisation’sstaff in the finance team.

4

Compliance with CBN policy of automating payments (straight through processing) and Financial Inclusion.
